noticeably better and actually fits the routine better — mix Saturday
evening, bake Sunday morning. Worked out cost per loaf at about £1.10
(flour, salt, energy), so £5 leaves a healthy margin and matches what
neighbours already pay for supermarket "bakery" bread.

## Decisions

  list.
